Meet Tarsha Campbell

Bio

TARSHA CAMPBELL is a freelance graphic designer with years of experience in the creative industry. Her work experience with international, national and local, entrepreneurs, non-profit organizations and businesses has granted her rewarding opportunities to showcase her expertise in graphic design and publishing. As a strategic creative thinker, Tarsha has a solution-oriented approach to graphic design coupled with a spirit of excellence. With a solid background in creative design services and publishing, she has spearheaded numerous creative projects from conception to fulfillment that has greatly benefited the businesses, non-profit organizations she has served. Tarsha’s creative talent and work ethic has allowed her clients to advance and establish their brands in the marketplace with brilliance and professionalism. As a certified coach, business mentor and empowerment speaker, Tarsha is passionate about teaching other leaders how to establish themselves and their brand with creative excellence.
